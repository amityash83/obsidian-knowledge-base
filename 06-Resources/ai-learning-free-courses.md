# Free AI Courses

## Summary
This note is a curated list of free AI learning resources from major technology vendors and education platforms. It is intended to support structured upskilling across foundational AI, applied tooling, and platform-specific learning.

## Concepts
- Curated resource notes reduce time spent rediscovering trustworthy learning material.
- Official training portals are useful for vendor-specific tools, APIs, and certifications.
- A stable learning note can support onboarding, self-study, and future roadmap planning.
