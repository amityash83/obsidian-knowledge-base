## 📌 What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to simulate human intelligence such as:

* Learning
* Reasoning
* Decision-making
* Pattern recognition

👉 Simple:

> AI = Machines that can learn and make decisions

---

## 🔹 Types of AI (By Capability)

### 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)

* Designed for specific tasks
* Cannot generalize beyond training

**Examples:**

* Chatbots
* Recommendation systems
* Image recognition

---

### 2. General AI (AGI)

* Human-level intelligence
* Can perform any intellectual task

**Status:** Not achieved yet

---

### 3. Super AI

* Beyond human intelligence
* Hypothetical

---

## 🔹 Types of AI (By Function)

### Reactive Machines

* No memory
* Only current input

### Limited Memory

* Uses past data
* Most ML systems fall here

### Theory of Mind

* Understand emotions (future)

### Self-aware AI

* Conscious systems (future)

## 🔹 Core Components of AI

### 1. Data

Raw input (logs, text, images)

### 2. Algorithms

Rules/models to learn patterns

### 3. Model

Trained system

### 4. Training

Learning from data

### 5. Inference

Using model in real-world

### 6. Feedback Loop

Improvement cycle

### 7. Infrastructure

* Compute (CPU/GPU)
* Storage
* Pipelines (MLOps)
